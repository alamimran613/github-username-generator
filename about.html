📖 How to Use

Step 1: Check Availability
Enter your desired username in the "Check Availability" panel and click the button. The tool will instantly tell you if it's available on GitHub.

Step 2: Generate Ideas
If your username is taken, use the "Generate Usernames" panel. Enter your name or keywords, and the tool will create unique suggestions for you.

Step 3: Copy & Use
Found the perfect username? Click the "Copy" button next to any generated username to copy it instantly!

💡 GitHub Username Best Practices

• Keep it professional - Your username represents you in the developer community

• Make it memorable - Short, easy-to-spell names are easier for others to remember

• Use your real name - Consider using your actual name or a variation for personal branding

• Avoid numbers when possible - Only alphanumeric characters and hyphens are allowed

• Length matters - GitHub usernames can be 1-39 characters long

• Think long-term - Choose a username you'll be happy with for years to come
